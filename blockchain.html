Blockchain network

With the blockchain to manage a mobility is much easier, since both unversities involved with a particular mobility holds a copy of the participants information.

Whenever there is a change in the information that was not intended to happen, the tight smart contracts protect these from happening. Enhancing system robustness. Better and faster issues processing.

Avoid tampered information to enter into the system, any fraudulent activity will be explicitly visible in the blockchain. Remove data counterfeiting in the exchange mobility flow.

Information that is exchanged about participants is confidential. The blockchain helps universities protect mobility information with the best cryptography techniques the industry has seen today.

Paperwork reduction. The blockchain helps universities by validating every single mobility artifact with tight consensus algorithms that ensures validity.

Increase trust among educational institutions. Trust is the single biggest factor that can make or break a business. The blockchain technology is all about trust.
